## Pending

### [2026-03-15] Event Scope — Post-Conference Plans Are a Distinct Event Type
- **Insight:** The "evening social" and "post-conference plan" are two different things for the same event day. The social is the main ticketed event. The post-conference plan is a follow-on experience — dinner + entertainment — that flows naturally after the conference ends.
- **Affected Skills:** events-team-orchestrator, venue-researcher
- **Suggested Improvement:** Add "post-conference experience" as a distinct event type in the intake checklist.

### [2026-03-15] Venue Research — Two-Phase Evenings Work Well for Corporate Groups
- **Insight:** A two-phase format (dinner at one venue, entertainment/activity at a second venue) works very well for corporate leadership groups of 25–30. It gives natural pacing.
- **Affected Skills:** venue-researcher, retreat-planner
- **Suggested Improvement:** When planning post-conference evenings, default to a two-phase format recommendation.

### [2026-03-06] Venue Research — Outside Catering Is the Primary Filter
- **Insight:** "Does this venue allow outside caterers?" is the single most important question — more than vibe, more than capacity.
- **Affected Skills:** venue-researcher, kosher-expert
- **Suggested Improvement:** Make outside catering status the FIRST thing to verify in research, before building out full venue profiles.

### [2026-03-06] Venue Research — Denver Closures Are High
- **Insight:** Many Denver venues researched were permanently closed. Denver hospitality has had significant turnover.
- **Affected Skills:** venue-researcher
- **Suggested Improvement:** When researching any city, add an explicit "verify operational status" step. Check Google Maps "Permanently Closed" flag and recent reviews before building a venue profile.

### [2026-03-06] Client Comms — BCC Format Is Preferred
- **Insight:** Daniel confirmed he wants to BCC all venues simultaneously rather than individual tailored emails.
- **Affected Skills:** web-inquiry-specialist
- **Suggested Improvement:** Default to BCC format for multi-venue outreach.

### [2026-03-06] Client Preferences — Notes Column Ownership
- **Insight:** The Notes column in the Outreach Tracker is personal space for the client. When we pre-filled it with research notes, they corrected us.
- **Affected Skills:** documentation-specialist
- **Suggested Improvement:** Notes column in Outreach Tracker = always blank, always.

### [2026-03-06] Venue Research — "Rugged/Authentic" Is a Distinct Category
- **Insight:** There is a meaningful difference between "nice bar" and "rugged/authentic." For hosts specifying rugged/grungy/authentic, check: founding year, local reputation, interior aesthetic, whether the venue has a history worth talking about.
- **Affected Skills:** venue-researcher
- **Suggested Improvement:** Add "authenticity filter" as a named step in venue evaluation.

### [2026-03-06] Client Context — Hotel Should Not Appear in Outreach
- **Insight:** The hotel name should never appear in venue outreach emails. Venue operators don't need to know where the group is staying.
- **Affected Skills:** web-inquiry-specialist
- **Suggested Improvement:** Standard rule: outreach emails reference the event date, guest count, and requirements only. No hotel name.
